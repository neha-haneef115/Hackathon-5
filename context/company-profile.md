# TechCorp Company Profile

## Overview
TechCorp is a B2B project management SaaS platform competing with Asana and Monday.com, helping teams collaborate more efficiently through intuitive task management, project tracking, and team communication tools.

## Business Model
- **Starter Plan**: $29/month (up to 5 users)
- **Pro Plan**: $99/month (up to 25 users)  
- **Enterprise Plan**: $499/month (unlimited users)

## Company Details
- **Founded**: 2021
- **Headquarters**: Austin, Texas
- **Team Size**: 85 employees
- **Customer Base**: 500+ business customers, ranging from SMB to mid-market companies

## Core Features
- **Task Management**: Create, assign, and track tasks with priorities and deadlines
- **Project Timelines**: Visual project planning with Gantt charts and timeline views
- **Team Chat**: Real-time messaging and collaboration within projects
- **File Storage**: Secure file sharing and document management
- **Dashboards**: Customizable analytics and reporting dashboards

## Key Integrations
- Slack
- Google Drive
- GitHub
- Zapier
- Jira

## Support SLA
- **AI Response**: Under 5 minutes
- **Human Escalation**: Under 2 hours for complex issues

## Target Market
TechCorp focuses on small to medium-sized businesses (SMBs) and mid-market companies that need powerful project management tools without the complexity of enterprise solutions. Our customers typically have teams between 5-100 employees and operate in technology, consulting, marketing, and professional services industries.

## Competitive Advantages
- Intuitive user interface with minimal learning curve
- Competitive pricing compared to enterprise solutions
- Fast AI-powered customer support
- Robust integration ecosystem
- Reliable uptime and performance
